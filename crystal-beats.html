Crystal Beats

Sound Puzzle LP

About the release

Another jigsaw puzzle full of sounds and beats. The album’s title is based on more used sounds of beating or pulsing riffs with a rhythmic atmosphere.

With 14 tracks divided into three parts of this album brings themes which stands for different ways of how music can be structured from sounds with natural rif, but with added harmony to fit the general sound scaping during each track.

I hope they will please you and you will have a good time with them not only about how the sound can be gradually changed but also about what rhythms can be created with it.
